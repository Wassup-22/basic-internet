10 Rules to Keep In Mind:

  • Always use respectful language when talking on the internet
  • Dont use all caps when typing
  • Think before you post
  • Be aware of who you talk to
  • Don't give out personal information
  • Don't Post Long Messages
  • Don't start rants or flame wars
  • Remember not say too many personal things about you
  • Spend some time talking to people in real life
  • Don't send large files to people
